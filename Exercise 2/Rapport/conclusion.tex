\chapter{Conclusion}
The expansion of a piezoelectric element has been measured using a Michelson Inteferometer.
The intensity was measured as a function of voltage over the piezo, 
and from this the expansion as a function of voltage could be acquired from fitting.
Different fitting models were tested, simplest of which was the linear model.
This model fit to the observations well, 
even though it is fundamentally flawed as it cannot account for piezoelectric hysteresis.
Although a third-degree polynomial model was attempted, the fitting algorithmn was not able to create a satisfying fit.
The second degree polynomial was therefore used as a middle ground, and it gave the best results.
